South Sudan Schools Debating Championship (SSSDC)

Juba Schools Debating Championship - Central Equatoria State

The Juba Schools Debating Championship serves as a regional qualifier for schools in Central Equatoria State. It brings together some of the brightest young minds from Juba and its surrounding areas to compete in an atmosphere of collaboration and intellectual rigor.

Through this championship, students are challenged to research, analyze, and debate complex topics ranging from governance and economic development to social issues affecting South Sudan and the world at large. The event also serves as a networking opportunity for students and educators, encouraging the exchange of ideas and best practices in education and debate.

Bor Schools Debating Championship - Jonglei State

The Bor Schools Debating Championship focuses on fostering debate excellence among students in Jonglei State. This event highlights the unique challenges and opportunities faced by the region while emphasizing the importance of dialogue in building a better future.

Participants from Bor and neighboring areas engage in debates that reflect their lived experiences and the aspirations of their communities. The championship aims to inspire a new generation of leaders who can articulate their vision for a prosperous South Sudan, rooted in peace, cooperation, and innovation.
